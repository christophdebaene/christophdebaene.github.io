    A fresh start

    After a decade-long hiatus, I've made the decision to revive my blog about my passion for technology and to share my experiences. Moreover, this site serves as my personal wiki, a digital repository where I document the tools and insights integral to my daily life as a technical consultant."

    I began blogging in 2004 and utilized DasBlog at that time. I created my own theme called Business which gained popularity in the blogosphere and contributed to a multi-user setup — DasBlog 1.9 released.

    For a long time, I used DasBlog but eventually I switched to Wordpress

    I decided to make a fresh start because much of the content from that time has become obsolete. I opted to use MkDocs to create a beautiful documentation site.

    I also converted my old blog site to MkDocs which is accessible here. To facilitate the transformation, I used wordpress-export-to-markdown to have an initial head start and made some adaptations afterward.
